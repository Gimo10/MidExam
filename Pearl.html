Pearl Farm

Pearl Farm Beach Resort was once home to a pearl farm, where precious pearls were nurtured and cultivated as the ocean's most beautiful gems. Enjoy a refreshing swim in our infinity pool with the exceptional view of Mount Apo. Bask in an open space on a bright sunny day or a moonlit evening. From jet skiing to diving, banana boating and fly boarding, you will definitely have a splash! We also offer non-motorized activities such as kayaking, hobie-kayaking, snorkeling, and paddle-boarding for anyone who loves sounder pursuits.
