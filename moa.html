Mall Of Asia

SM Mall of Asia, also abbreviated as SM MoA, or simply Mall of Asia or MoA, is a large shopping mall in the Philippines, located at Bay City, Pasay, Philippines, within the SM Central Business Park, a reclaimed area within Manila Bay, and the southern end of Epifanio de los Santos Avenue (EDSA). SM Mall of Asia has a 2-storey entertainment complex with world-class movie theatres and contains the country's first Olympic-sized ice-skating rink. The extensive selection of shops and dining establishments, from fast food to gourmet, can be a bit overwhelming.
