Ariel's Point

Ariel's Point is in Buruanga, on the island of Panay. This small island welcomes its guests with their blue waters, relaxing tropical ambiance with different species of flora and fauna around. There are no buildings, or vast commercial spaces built that complete an escape-from-the-city experience for their visitors.
