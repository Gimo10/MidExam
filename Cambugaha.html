Cambugahay Falls

Cambugahay Falls is one of the most popular tourist spots in all of Siquijor. It is a three-tiered waterfall with a large natural pool at the bottom tier where people can enjoy the multiple rope swings, rubber rings, and rafts. Relax With Nature · Swim in the Cold Soothing Water · Do Some Waterfalls Jumping · Try the Tarzan Swing · Ride the Bamboo Rafter ·
