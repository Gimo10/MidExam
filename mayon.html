Mayon Volcano

Mayon, also known as Mount Mayon and Mayon Volcano, is an active stratovolcano is part of a national park & has erupted numerous times since 2000 in the province of Albay in Bicol, Philippines. A popular tourist spot, it is renowned for its "perfect cone" because of its symmetric conical shape, and is regarded as very sacred in Philippine mythology.
