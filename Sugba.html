Sugba Lagoon

Sugba Lagoon is one of Siargao's top rated destinations. Almost completely enclosed in a small island off of Siargao, the water in the lagoon is this outrageous blue-green. From the air, Sugba Lagoon resembles a strange maze of vibrant water separated by small islands and domed mountains. Sugba Lagoon is a hidden gem and a must-visit when in Siargao. It can be reached within less than an hour by boat from Siargao and is definitely worth a trip. This lagoon offers the most transparent and turquoise water, stand-up paddleboarding, kayaking, and much more.
