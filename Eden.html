Eden Nature Park

With an elevation of 3,000 meters above sea level and a canopy of trees blocking out some of the sun's rays, the park is also refreshingly cool, giving visitors a respite from the heat of the metro. There are lots of activities you can do at Eden Nature Park! The most famous to date is the Skywheel, Skycycle and skyswing. ATTRACTIONS · Amphitheater · Birdwalk · Deer Park · Flower Garden · Hiking Trail · Lola's Garden · Hydroponics · Playground.
