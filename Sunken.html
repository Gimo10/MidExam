Sunken Cementry

As its name implies, this burial ground is located underneath the waves off the northwestern coast of Camiguin. Its spot is marked by a large cross that you can spot from Camiguin. To view the cemetery, most travelers ride a van bound for Mt. Vulcan, where they can get an unobstructed view of the Sunken Cemetery.
