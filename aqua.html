Aqua Planet

Aqua Planet called the god of heaven accepted her request and the water planet was named Halia Aqua Planet. This one is exciting beyond your imagination. Widely considered the most extreme ride at the park. While standing vertically in a tube, flaps suddenly open dropping the participant feet-first into a 100-meter fall that traverses into a 360-degree loop. Everyone from child to adult can feel comfortable while allowing for an enhanced level of excitement for the thrill seekers in the group. Each attraction has a minimum and maximum weight and height requirement.
