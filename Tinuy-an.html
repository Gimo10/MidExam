Tinuy-an Falls

Located on the island of Mindanao in the Philippines, Tinuy-an Falls is a multi-tiered waterfall said to be the widest in the country. Tinuy-an Falls cascades over three tiers, with the second being the largest and most impressive. Tinuy-an is a white water curtain that flows in three levels (with a fourth tier hidden from view) and is said to be the widest waterfall in the Philippines.
