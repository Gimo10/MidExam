Pusan Point

Pusan Point is one of the several gorgeous spots hidden in the town of Caraga in Davao Oriental. It is the place where the light hits first during sunrise as it is the easternmost part of the country. Points are pieces of land that project into a body of water. The Pusan Point Eco-Park features two (2) old lighthouses, a Jubilee cross, a Meditation kiosk, Amphitheater, a swimming pool, a jogging lane, and a resting area. The park also allowed Camping for those who want to spend their nights watching the next day's sunrise.
