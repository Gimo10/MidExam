Boracay

Boracay, one of the top Islands in the Philippines, is popular for its beaches and blessed with a long stretch of powdery white sand, crystal clear and azure waters, and a stunning sunset. Boracay is the most iconic island in the Philipines and has won multiple awards in previous years. The island boasts of crystal azure waters, powdery white sand, abundant flora and fauna, and diverse marine life. No wonder it remains as one of the best islands in the world and was recognized by Conde Nast Traveler and Travel+Leisure
