Kalanggaman Island

Kalanggaman Island is situated in the beautiful landscape of Palompon, Leyte, Philippines. It is renowned for its clear blue waters, white pulverulent sand, and its elongated sandbars that surround both sides of the majestic island. Kalanggaman Island is found in the municipality west of Ormoc City.
