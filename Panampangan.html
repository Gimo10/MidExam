Panampangan Island

Panampangan Island is an island in the municipality of Sapa-Sapa, Tawi-Tawi. With an area of 0.1 square kilometres (0.039 sq mi). It is located inside Basibuli Shoal. The island is situated along the Basibuli Shoal on the Celebes Sea, the calm, shallow waters of the island are perfect for snorkeling and swimming. With out the crowd of other popular beach destinations in the Philippines, visitors can enjoy the quiet sounds of the beach without the noise of crowds. Things to do. Snorkeling and Swimming · Collecting seashells · Beach Walk · Relax in one of the Huts.
