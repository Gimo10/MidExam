Baggao

Inside the cave, you will see an Olympic-sized natural pool with enchanting stalactites and stone formations. The overall experience definitely deserves a spot in anyone's bucket list. Baggao is gifted with natural aesthetically pristine and beautiful sceneries where horizons of water, rocks, rare species of plants and untamed animals abound. The lush of rolling hills and mountains covered with thick forest vegetation is evident of truly awesome and breathtaking gift of Mother Nature!
