Kawasan Falls

It has three waterfall levels, all of which feature natural pools where visitors can swim and relax. Kawasan is not only picturesque, but it is also an excellent place to swim in. Visitors often praise the water as cold and refreshing. The water from the top of Kawasan comes from the Kandayvic Spring. ‘Canyoneering’, also known as ‘canyoning’, refers to traveling down a canyon through a mix of activities like climbing, hiking, cliff diving, swimming and other similar fun activities.
