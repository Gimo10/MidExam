Windmills

Known as the first windfarm in Southeast Asia, Ilocos Norte’s beautiful Bangui Windmill Farm is a must-see. With 26 turbines to date and counting, the Ilocos Region’s first and largest wind farm is a magnificent sight to behold. The “Windmills” supply more than 40% of Ilocos Norte’s electricity through its connection to the Luzon Grid. It is of great pleasure for PTS to be a part of this milestone in the renewable energy sector in the Philippines.
