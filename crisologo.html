Crisologo Museum

Crisologo Museum is an ancestral house turned into a museum by the Crisologos, who happens to be one of Vigan's most prominent political family figure. The Crisologo museum is one of the most famous tourist attraction in Vigan City, Ilocos Sur. Primarily, its fame is related to its well-known owner as well as the content which brings you back to the Spanish era.
