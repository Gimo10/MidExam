Tinago Falls

The falls is high, its very cold waters cascading beautifully into a deep and calm basin-like pool which appears like a blue-colored lagoon. It is considered as one of the main tourist attractions in Iligan. And is included in the list of the 'Majestic Waterfalls' in the city. Good spot for swimming and cliff diving. For a complete waterfall experience, bamboo rafts are made available for rent.
